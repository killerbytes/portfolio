## Overview

The **Inventory & Sales Management System** is a full-featured enterprise frontend application engineered for retail and warehouse operations. It streamlines inventory auditing, product price tracking, order creation, and movement logging.

Designed with **ReactJS (Functional Components + Custom Hooks)**, **TypeScript (Strict)**, and **Tailwind CSS**, the platform delivers an intuitive, fast dashboard experience with responsive mobile views.

---

## Key Features

1. **Interactive Analytics Dashboard**: Live metrics for Total Revenue, Low Stock Alerts, Active Sales Orders, and Fast-Moving Items.
2. **Product Multi-Variants & Combinations**: Users can seamlessly create, manage, and track inventory for products with multiple variants (e.g., size, color, material) and dynamically generate all possible combinations with unique SKUs, barcodes, and pricing structures.
3. **Product Price & Stock Audit History**: Historical tracking of price adjustments, inventory restocks, and warehouse transfers.
4. **Sales Order Builder**: Streamlined order creation workflow with instant cart total calculations, customer selection, and validation.
5. **Mobile Responsive UI**: Custom mobile drawer menus and touch-optimized data tables for on-the-go warehouse staff.

---

## Technical Highlights & Architecture

- **Custom Hook Optimization**: Engineered memory-stable custom hook logic preventing infinite re-render loops during date recalculations.
- **Component-Driven Design**: Clean separation of concerns with reusable UI cards, data tables, modal dialogs, and badge components.
- **Accessibility**: Built with high contrast standards (WCAG 2.1), ARIA labels, and full keyboard navigation support.
